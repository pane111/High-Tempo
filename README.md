# High Tempo
 A game about going fast. Run, jump, dash and slash your way to the end!
 

# CONTROLS:


* W - Move forward
* S - Move backwards
* Space - Jump
* Space in midair - Dash
* Right click (hold) - Shoot (Release when drone is fully charged to perfrom laser beam)
* Left click - Attack
* Left Shift - Backstep
* A/D - Sidestep
